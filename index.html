Welcome to our Farm


Have you ever experienced the joy of a truly fresh bouquet of flowers? Flowers that were cut just hours (or minutes) before they landed on your table? We have here at our "urban farm" (really, it's just our backyard) and we want to share all the with you! We have big plans to grow buckets and buckets of blooms. Please follow us to find out when and where you can get your hands on some! We know you will love them as much as we do.
